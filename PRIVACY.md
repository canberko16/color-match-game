# Privacy Policy — Color Match

Last updated: April 2026

Color Match does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

## Data We Collect

- **Game scores**: Stored locally on your device only (AsyncStorage). Never transmitted.
- **Matchmaking**: Temporary anonymous queue entries in Firebase Realtime Database, auto-deleted on disconnect and immediately after match.

## What We Do NOT Collect

- Name, email, or any account information
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs
- Location data
- Contact list or photos
- Payment information

## Firebase Realtime Database

Competitive matchmaking uses Firebase Realtime Database (Google LLC). Only an anonymous session key and your current trophy count are stored temporarily during matchmaking. This data is deleted automatically when you disconnect or when a match is found.

Firebase Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

## Children's Privacy

Color Match is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
